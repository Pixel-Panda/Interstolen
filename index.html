Projects

AwesomeSource

While finding open-source projects to contribute to, people have to go through the struggle of finding something which suits them. The people who maintain the project, or the project owner(s), have to go through the struggle of increasing the popularity of their project to try and get folks to find out and contribute! AwesomeSource solves all of that. AwesomeSource is a website where people can find and post open-source projects, and it simplifies the process of contributing to open-source even more!

SnipBin

SnipBin is a elegant, minimalist, and blazing fast code pasting tool which was built as an alternative to PasteBin. There aren't many great ways to share code snippets in today's world. I wanted to build a tool which was simple, wasn't cluttered with bad UI or ads, and easy to use. That's why I made SnipBin. SnipBin has a simplified design, no need for a thousand things like just to share a code snippet, SnipBin keeps everything simple and to the point. It has a simple and dark UI which is comfy for the eyes to look at, and syntax highlighting for over 80 languages, all built-in.

SketchMessage

Unlike boring messaging apps, SketchMessage changes the game. It is an app where you can communicate with people all over the world by drawing on a whiteboard. This is an elegant, unique and cool project made over a week with the intention of making talking with other humans over screens less boring.
